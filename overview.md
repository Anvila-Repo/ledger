# Overview

## Purpose
Ledger exists to simplify the daily habit of financial tracking. By removing the friction of manual spreadsheet data entry, Ledger transforms raw user inputs (e.g., "spent $45 on dinner with friends last night") into an organized, categorized, and actionable financial ledger.

## What Ledger Does
* **Real-time Logging:** Parses daily spending inputs and organizes them by merchant, amount, category, and date.
* **Budget Management:** Tracks spending against user-defined limits and visualizes remaining discretionary funds.
* **Trend Identification:** Highlights recurring subscriptions, spikes in specific categories, or unexpected savings opportunities.
* **Encouragement:** Celebrates financial milestones (e.g., sticking to a weekly dining-out budget) to reinforce positive behavior.
